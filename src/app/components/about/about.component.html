About Decentralized Betting Platform Developed by Alex Decentralized peer-to-peer betting platform, built on the Ethereum Blockchain.
The user bets just agains the rest of participants of the network without third party involved.
Currently there are 3 options to bet in a match: victory local team, victory away team or draw.
No company put the odds. The maths are the ones in charge of distribute the money when the match finishes:
All the winners will get all the money bet in the match, while the losers just lose that money. .

There is not third party involved, which makes it totally decentralized.
